About Us

We are a performing arts studio servicing the Henderson, Southern Las Vegas and Boulder City areas. We offer a variety of courses for kids ages 4 to 18 years. Our studio has a fully equipped theatre as well as additional rooms for dance and vocal performance classes.

Our Mission

Seedling Theatre Company is dedicated to providing an environment where children and teens feel welcome, encouraged, and empowered through participating in the performing arts and where instructors teach with patience and love, striving to develop the potential in every student.

Classes

The main goals of this theatre company are fulfilled each week in the "classroom" settings of our semester-long courses and week-long summer camps. With carefully selected teaching teams of experienced instructors, directors, and choreographers, your child is in good hands.
